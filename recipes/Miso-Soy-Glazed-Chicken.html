Miso Soy Glazed Chicken Thighs

Juicy and umami flavors explode in this Miso Soy Glazed Chicken Thighs, paired perfectly with julienned scallions and cucumbers.

The unexpected collaboration of miso, soy sauce, and honey will make you want more and more!

Ingredients

  • chicken thighs with skin
  • green onions/scallions
  • miso paste
  • cucumber
  • soy sauce
  • honey
  • garlic
  • mirin

Instructions

  1. make rice
  2. slice cucumbers and mince 3-4 cloves of garlic
  3. julienne (cut into long thin strips) green onions and soak in water to make it less spicier
  4. to make the sauce, add to a small bowl (proportions): miso paste (2), honey (2), soy sauce (2), mirin (1), water (until slightly thick consistency), minced garlic
  5. score your chicken thigh skin
  6. grill chicken on pan (med-high heat) skin side first
  7. once chicken is slightly crispy and both sides change color, add the glaze and keep cooking/flipping until sauce is thick and skin is crispy
  8. add chicken to a bowl of fresh rice
  9. top with cucumbers and drained green onions/scallions
  10. drizzle with hot honey on top to taste/preference
