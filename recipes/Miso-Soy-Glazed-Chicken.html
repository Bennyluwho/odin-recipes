Soba Noodles

I will walkthrough the easy process of creating Soba Noodles, a deliciously simple Japanese cuisine.

The dish gives a cold noodle with a sweet and tangy taste that will make you crave more every time you eat it!

Ingredients

  • soba noodles
  • soba tsuyu
  • cucumbers
  • lime (or lemon)
  • green onions

Instructions

  1. Boil water in cook pot
  2. Once water boils, add soba noodles to portion and cook according to packaged instructions
  3. Slice cucumbers into thin strips (depending on texture preference)
  4. Chop green onions for ganish
  5. A slice of lemon or lime
  6. Once noodles are done, wash in cold water until water runs relatively clear
  7. Add tsuyu and water into the bowl of noodles
  8. Top with cucumbers, green onions, and squeeze in lemon/lime juice
