Honey Garlic Salmon Bites

A moderately hard dish to make, the Honey Garlic Salmon Bites is a worthy dish to make for the family and will fulfill with ease.

Usually served topped with grilled grean beans and a rice base, covered in the same sauce as the salmon. This dish has a good amount of sweet and garlic taste to ensure a satisfactory bite with every scoop!

Ingredients

  • green onions
  • skinless salmon
  • green beans
  • soy sauce
  • honey
  • garlic
  • mirin

Instructions

  1. make rice
  2. cut off green bean ends (about 1/2 lb) and cut in half/bite-sized pieces
  3. chop green onions for garnish
  4. mince 3-4 cloves of garlic
  5. cut salmon (about 1.5 lb size) into bite-sized cubes
  6. Ina a mixing bowl, make sauce (spoonful proportions): soy sauce (2), honey(2), mirin(1), minced garlic, water (about 1/2 of the sauce already combined)
  7. after sauce is mixed, add the salmon cubes and mix it in
  8. on low-med heat, add the salmon mix to pan
  9. once cooked, garnish with green onions
  10. eat with fresh bowl of rice
